About the Game

Don't Starve Together is a multiplayer survival game that thrusts you into a bleak, unforgiving wilderness where every decision can mean the difference between life and death. Traverse a beautifully hand-drawn, gothic world filled with bizarre creatures, shifting seasons, and harsh elements.

With an emphasis on resource management, crafting, and exploration, each playthrough offers a unique blend of strategy and chance. Whether you team up with friends or brave the dangers alone, the journey is as unpredictable as it is thrilling.

Meet the Survivors

Each character in Don't Starve Together is a unique survivor, armed with distinct abilities and rich backstories. Choose the character that best fits your playstyle and embark on a perilous adventure in a world where every step could be your last.

From the resourceful scientist Wilson to the enigmatic, fire-loving Willow, discover the diverse cast of characters that add depth and personality to your survival experience.
